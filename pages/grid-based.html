Grid Based Layout - Poems

Graphic and web-designers work in the dimension that can be broken down to the rectangle, be it a page or a screen. This determines the shape of the grid as well. The lines within the grid are customized. Creators can build the grid themselves or rely on ready grid systems, while designers can use the same grid system throughout the whole piece of design, or create a new grid for each page to express their ideas. Grid Layout Design History at Elekan.co

Having a Great Time Being Transgender in America Lately

BY JACKIE SABBAGH

It is day infinity
of everyone wanting me dead. People are having fun bringing lemon squares and automatic artillery to the anti-trans community meetings.
Divorced legislators harangue
about pedophile cults and surgeried infants and what ever happened to forever ago.
I am more beautiful than you and I would like to be loved.
I am getting concerned
about the monomaniacs who make their entire lives about deadnaming and transvestigations:
obviously it's working but aren't you exhausted, don't you remember
when someone loved you without knowing what you were?
I am eating shortbread on a patio table overlooking the enormous green ocean.
Somewhere an octopus is being eaten by an octopus and not panicking.
Black dress to the floor, red acrylic nails, silver teardrop earrings, waterproof mascara.
I am excited to do this for the rest of my life and be terrified.
I hear a noise behind me and I don't turn around.

If You're Not Planning on Going to Heaven, go to Norway

BY D. COFFYN

Which is my taxi driver's way of saying, Marvel while you are on Earth.
We do not know what awaits us hereafter. Befriend every dog.
Trace calloused fingertips along the wrinkled wisdom of a Redwood until they soften.
Bask in the rapture of a cicada symphony.
Allow peach cobbler drenched in vanilla cream to dribble down your unplucked chin.
Eat tomatoes from the vine. Become the patron saint of fireflies. Stargaze in Yellowstone.
Drive through the night to reach salt-licked shores.
Savor the brine of a sweating kiss.
Be generous with I love you.
Inhale joy. Exhale sorrow.
Absorb all the beauty of being alive.
